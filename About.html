About Me

I graduated from the University of Houston with two Bachelors of Business Administration degrees, one in Marketing and one in Management. I have held positions in various marketing fields, including social media, content marketing, and digital marketing. I use visual design principles, such as the use of color, typography, and layouts to create visually stunning marketing content, tailored to the needs of each individual client. I have a deep understanding of responsive design principles, enabling me to create websites that adapt seamlessly to different devices and screen sizes. I am well versed in SEO and accessibility principals, as well. I have extensive experience using all of the major CMS platforms such as Shopify, Wix, and WordPress. However, I also know HTML and CSS. I also have beginners' knowledge of JavaScript, PHP, and Json. I combine my marketing skills with my web design skills to create websites that are not just visually appealing but also user-friendly and optimized for performance. By staying at the forefront of design trends and continuously expanding my skill set, I provide solutions that leave a lasting impact.
