About Me

I graduated from the University of Houston with a BBA in Marketing and a BBA in Management. Afterwards, I worked in a variety of marketing roles, including social media marketing, content marketing, and digital marketing. I combine my marketing skills with my web design skills to create the perfect websites that are visually appealing, user-friendly, and optimized for performance. I can also align the client's marketing goals with the website's design. For example, I use my understanding of branding to create a website that reflects a company's brand identity.

I use visual design principles, such as the use of color, typography, and layouts to create visually stunning, user-friendly websites, tailored to the needs of each individual client. I have a deep understanding of responsive design principles, enabling me to create websites that adapt seamlessly to different devices and screen sizes.

By staying at the forefront of design trends and continuously expanding my skill set, I provide comprehensive web design solutions that leave a lasting impact.
