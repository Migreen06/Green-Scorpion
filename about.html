We are a small, dedicated team of winners offering a full range of custom web development web design, digital marketing, and graphic design.

How We Got Started

Green Scorpion was started in Houston, Texas by Michelle Green and her refusal to settle for a mediocre digital ads, repetitive templatized websites, and agencies that suck. After double-majoring in Business at the University of Houston and grinding for 15 years as a Director of Marketing, Michelle spent her "free time" mastering the gut-level technicality of Web Development. She didn't just learn to code; she learned to weaponize it.

What We do

We’re here because we’re tired of the "seas of sameness" the endless, bloated templates and lazy "plug-and-play" shortcuts that make every brand look like a carbon copy of its neighbor. At Green Scorpion, we color outside the lines because the lines are where businesses go to die. We are code-first artisans who believe your digital identity should be a lightning-fast, hand-coded masterpiece, not a drag-and-drop afterthought.



What We Don't Do

We don't do "safe." We don't do generic. If you're looking for a "yes-man" agency to slap a logo on a $20 WordPress theme and call it a day, you’re in the wrong place. We don't recycle code, we don't follow tired industry trends, and we certainly don't play by the rules that have kept your competitors stagnant.



Our Mission

To incinerate digital mediocrity by merging high-level creative production with deep-stack technical excellence via various forms of web design, web development, marketing, and graphic design.
