About
Us

Green Scorpion was started in Houston, fueled by Michelle Green’s refusal to settle for a mediocre digital landscape.

How We Got Started

After double-majoring in Business at the University of Houston and grinding for 15 years as a Director of Marketing, Michelle spent her "free time" mastering the gut-level technicality of Web Development. She didn't just learn to code; she learned to weaponize it.



What We do

We’re here because we’re tired of the "seas of sameness" the endless, bloated templates and lazy "plug-and-play" shortcuts that make every brand look like a carbon copy of its neighbor. At Green Scorpion, we color outside the lines because the lines are where businesses go to die. We are code-first artisans who believe your digital identity should be a lightning-fast, hand-coded masterpiece, not a drag-and-drop afterthought.



What We Don't Do

We don't do "safe." We don't do generic. If you're looking for a "yes-man" agency to slap a logo on a $20 WordPress theme and call it a day, you’re in the wrong place. We don't recycle code, we don't follow tired industry trends, and we certainly don't play by the rules that have kept your competitors stagnant.
