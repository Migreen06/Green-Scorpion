Large Scale Product Launch

The project was complex, involving 24 distinct SKUs; to prevent choice fatigue and optimize the customer experience, I strategically segmented the launch into two lines based on wood type. As the sole driver of the strategy, I took full responsibility for the entire digital lifecycle, from initial creative production to final conversion analysis.

  • Strategy & Planning:
    Developed a comprehensive digital strategy that aligned with the brand's unique value proposition and target audience.

  • Creative Production:
    Directed end-to-end production, including digital asset design, YouTube video production, and all campaign copywriting.

  • Conversion Strategy: Built a custom landing page optimized to capture leads and streamline the conversion funnel.

  • Acquisition & Reach: Managed a multi-channel ad strategy across Google, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok, and X to drive brand awareness.

  • Data-Driven Optimization:Leveraged analytics to monitor user engagement and refine ad spend in real-time for maximum ROI.

The campaign successfully drove over $1M in revenue and acquired thousands of new customers.
